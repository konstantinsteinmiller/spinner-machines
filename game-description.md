# Spinner Machines

**Pull. Launch. CHAOS.**

Fling your spinner into the stage with one simple swipe and watch the mayhem unfold!

## How to Play

Drag back, aim, and let go — that's it. One finger is all you need. Every launch sends your spinner flying across the
stage and destroys objects. reach the green exit gate to finish the level with minimum launches.

## What Makes It Awesome

- **20+ campaign stages** with boss fights and machines, that modify the way you traverse the stages. Push pressure
  plates, activate gears, use magnetic rails or gravity wells to move around corners. Machines are the way to success.
  Destroy generators, glass tubes and bosses for maximum score points per stage
- **45+ skins** with special effects like rainbow trails, sandstorms, and forest dragons breathing color across the
  arena
- **Battle Pass, daily rewards, treasure chests, and a prize roulette** keep the loot coming
- **Meteor showers and 3-2-1-GO countdowns** kick off stages with style

Easy to pick up, impossible to put down. Jump in, start flinging, and let the chaos begin.
